## Study Prompt for Agents

Use these websites as visual-reference material only. Do not clone layouts, assets, copy, brand systems, illustrations, or proprietary interactions.

For each site, extract:

1. Typography system
2. Color system
3. Layout grammar
4. Spacing rhythm
5. Component treatment
6. Motion language
7. Image or illustration style
8. Navigation structure
9. Content density
10. One distinctive design move
11. What makes it feel human-designed
12. What should not be copied directly

Then create a new visual system for the target product with its own original art direction.\
